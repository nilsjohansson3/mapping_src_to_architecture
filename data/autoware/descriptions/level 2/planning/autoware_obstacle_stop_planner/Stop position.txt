| Parameter                             | Type   | Description                                                                                                                                    |
| ------------------------------------- | ------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `max_longitudinal_margin`             | double | margin between obstacle and the ego's front [m]                                                                                                |
